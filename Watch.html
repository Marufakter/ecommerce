Rolex Submariner

Rolex Submariner

Overview

The Rolex Submariner is a classic diver's watch that has been in production since 1953. It is one of the most iconic watches in the world, and is known for its ruggedness, reliability, and timeless design. The Submariner is water resistant to a depth of 300 meters, and is equipped with a rotating bezel that allows divers to measure elapsed time underwater.

Features

  • Stainless steel case and bracelet
  • Black dial with luminous hour markers and hands
  • Date display at 3 o'clock
  • Rotating bezel with 60-minute scale
  • Automatic movement
  • Water resistant to 300 meters

Price

The Rolex Submariner has a retail price of $8,100.

Omega Seamaster

Omega Seamaster

Overview

The Omega Seamaster is a versatile and stylish watch that is equally at home on land or in the water. It is water resistant to a depth of 150 meters, and is equipped with a helium escape valve that allows for safe use during saturation diving.

Features

  • Stainless steel case and bracelet
  • Blue dial with luminous hour markers and hands
  • Date display at 6 o'clock
  • Rotating bezel with 60-minute scale
  • Automatic movement
  • Water resistant to 150 meters
  • Helium escape valve

Price

The Omega Seamaster has a retail price of $5,200.
