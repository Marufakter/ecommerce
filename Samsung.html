Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra

Overview

The Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra is the latest flagship smartphone from Samsung. It features a large 6.9-inch AMOLED display with a resolution of 1440 x 3200 pixels. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 895 processor and comes with 16GB of RAM and 512GB of internal storage. The device runs on Android 13 and is powered by a 5000mAh battery.

Camera

The Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra features a quad-camera setup on the back with a 108MP primary sensor, a 16MP ultra-wide sensor, a 12MP telephoto lens, and a 3D ToF sensor. The device also has a 40MP front-facing camera for selfies and video calls.

Connectivity

The Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra supports 5G connectivity and also comes with Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, NFC, and GPS. It has a USB Type-C port for charging and data transfer and also comes with a 3.5mm headphone jack.

Other Features

  • Fingerprint (under display, ultrasonic)
  • Samsung DeX
  • Bixby natural language commands and dictation
  • Fast charging 65W
  • Wireless charging 25W
  • Reverse wireless charging 15W
